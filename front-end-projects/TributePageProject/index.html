One Piece Characters

  • Monkey D. Luffy - He is the primary protagonist of the One Piece series. He wants to become King of the Pirates
  • Roronoa Zoro - He is a swordsman who uses up to three swords simultaneously, holding one in each hand and a third in his mouth. He wants to become world's greatest swordsman.
  • Nami - Nami becomes an excellent burglar, pickpocket, and navigator with an exceptional ability to forecast weather.
  • Usopp - He is a gifted inventor, painter, and sculptor.In combat, he relies primarily on slingshots to fire various kinds of ammunition with great precision. He achieves notoriety under his alter-ego Sniper King the King of Snipers
  • Sanji - He is a first-rate cook and learn kick-based fighting style. He never refuse a starving person a meal and becomes infamous under the name of Black Leg Sanji.
  • Tony Tony Chopper - He is a doctor and a blue-nosed reindeer. The power of the Zoan-type Human-Human Fruit provides him with the ability to transform into a reindeer or a reindeer hybrid.
  • Nico Robin - She becomes an archaeologist at the age of eight.She she acquires the outlawed knowledge of how to translate the ancient stones called Ponegliffs.
  • Franky - He is taken in as an apprentice by a shipwright who secretly holds the plans for a devastating ancient weapon.
  • Brook - He first enters the Grand Line as vice-captain of the music-themed Rumbar Pirates. He is an excellent musician, who says that he can play any instrument, although he is usually seen playing the violin. He can even influence people with his music to the point of making them fall asleep.
